# Introduction

Color is the widest and least divisible arbitrary category of existence that exists. That is to say that Color is the fundamental makeup of all things, as foundational as the sky and the earth. Though its facets vary widely in number for the various species of the world, for human beings, the Colors of reality divide mostly cleanly into seven.

## **[[Identity]]**

The Color of that which withstands all things alien to itself—the self, the soul, the origin, the hidden things—red as the blood in our veins and the dark light that illuminates our being.
![[font.png | center]]


  

To touch the world through its identity is to become the center of everything that happens, something so deeply fundamental to any thinking being that the perception of its relation to the greater world is almost impossible to attain without having one’s mind opened externally.

A full understanding of the nexuses of all things opens the path to every thing. Hear the whispers of unspoken secrets and touch the fleeting light at the heart of living beings. Unmake, undo, unfurl, unearth—where the other Colors reach outward, the Red pulls inexorably inward and back to the start.

---
## [[Entropy]]

Freedom in its purest form is not wild and feverish; it is vast and patient without end. It is the everlasting force that drives everything forward shimmers like burning embers.
![[flame.png | center]]
The past chars away to ashen memory as the present burns its way into the future. Uncovering the world and its unending entropy makes oneself a ceaseless arrow of time pointing toward the growing chaos. See countless futures unfold and emerge like fractal leaves and watch infinitesimal sparks of opportunity and chance accumulate until they become an unstoppable tide.

To become a navigator of the sprawling chaos is to become another cresting wave crashing on the shore of the tumid river. Walking the dark space between intention and action, one becomes the shadow. After the motion and before the action—on that fine line between will and what will be lies only you.

---

## **[[Continuity]]**

The rumor down the grapevine is that the unbroken thread with which fate weaves the tapestry of all lives shines like glittering gold.
![[dynamo.png | center]]

Through the criss-cross patterns do we traverse the entirety of the world. In perpendicular movements we surreptitiously pluck the delicate strings of time and space, making of them such pretty knots and loops, spun into the faces of a die or perhaps the curvature of space. Wrap the threads of others’ lives and intertwine them with our own, that we might remind everyone that we are once and still whole.

The tangled dance of life, the universe, and everything else is a dance rarely practiced and seldom mastered. Jaunt across the the seamless gaps and trapeze across the tightrope of universal connection. Do not worry, for in our faltering we shall fall into each other’s open embrace.

## [[Infinity]]

The only discernible thing about the heap of refuse at the end of the world is that its contents take on a faint green hue.

![[taboo.png | center]]


In Death’s kingdom, the indiscernible figures shuffle body-against body across the narrow passageways, packed like sardines in a crushed tin can, murmuring in incoherent grievances, and fading softly—without resistance—into the blending silhouette of the great and unending heap. Drift in this twilight kingdom; touch the faces in the interminable crowd; hear the cacophony of too many words and thoughts and prayers.

  
To delve deep into the dregs of the world and dredge from its depths that abyssal refuse is to risk becoming another permanent resident of that distant kingdom. Alas, the chance visitors continue to come, go, and stay, for when the face of reality offers nothing, the obscured shapes that undulate beneath its stifled underbelly may be the only thing resembling a glimpse of hope.

---

## [[Symmetry]]

Not even nothing escapes the purview of the Color of order itself, for even the most flagrant transgressions against the orthodox belongs to its shadowy dual.

![[river.png | center]]

Chaos, delirium, anarchy; all such things possess not a lack of order but an order of lacking. Look within the infinite symmetries and antisymmetries of the universe, and it shall elucidate your mechanisms like the collimated light of a lens. Every window is a mirror. Every entrance is a barrier. Motion without movement, actions without cause—paradoxes resolve to greater designs.

Looking past the one-way mirror that is awareness is merely to become aware of one’s place in the grand architecture of the universe. One does not act upon the system as an outsider: Even the capacity to change the inner workings of order is internal to order itself. The unchanging and unending symmetry is the sovereign of all that can be surveyed.

---
## [[Verity]]

The endless struggle for supremacy takes place upon many battlefields—the skies, the stars, the things we do—washing against the ragged coast of the future in foaming waves of deep dark indigo.

![[light.png | center]]

Throughout the heavens and earth, all things take their shape through violent erosion. The child supersedes the parent; the river cleaves the valley; the perpetrator conquers the victim. Violence creates distance, and distance is a weapon of self defense. What will one experience when they rise, high as the sky and lonely as a god? The unfettered self, and with it the realization of the crushing weight of the other.

To wield the truth is to take up arms against reality itself. It is an arrant declaration of a flower to prune the weeds. In your hand now is a knife—the first knife of knives. Take it, and carve your truth onto the face of reality like sharpened edge ripping through a gaping wound.

---

## [[Propriety]]

Through dazzling violet arcs and across countless domains, the walk of life unfurls its myriad multitudes.
![[purple.png | center]]


Hand by hand and step by step does one navigate the all-encompassing graph of existence. Each interchange of nodes brings the greater design closer into view, tracing the outlines of objects and traversing the interconnected web of interdependent lives. Scattered dots of meaning orbiting like abyss-adrift stars swirl in unseen gravities and pass each other gently in the night, tender as lovers’ kiss and transient as strangers’ exchange. The multitude of the minuscule form the whole, and the pattern emerges.

To define is to skip across the countless islands of meaning in the ocean of nothingness, creating companionship where there was loneliness and connection where there was isolation. Yet it is never the disparate nodes that grant meaning; it is the leap of faith, taken with open eyes and embracing arms across the impassable gap.
