Manifestation techniques are a class of cataphatic technique revolving around the direct reification of concepts into reality. Their primary application lies in the temporary reification of communicable signs that are typically unsustainable or realistically unachievable with current means, such as the manifestation of extraordinarily large scales of energy or exotic forms of matter.

  

---

## Basic Principles

### Unstable Product

Manifestation techniques specifically target concepts too difficult to bring into existence via other techniques. Unlike [[Projection Technique]], which target concepts incapable of manifesting in real space, manifestation techniques’ products _can_ exist, but are typically too exotic or exhaustive to reify otherwise. As such, the reified product is universally unstable and incapable of existing on its own for significant periods of time, requiring stabilization via auxiliary techniques.

**A manifestation technique shortcuts the signs required to produce the goal.**

The instability of manifestation techniques means their greatest weaknesses are percussive disruption techniques that cause their immediate collapse, though a skilled practitioner can make use of this weakness by exploiting the product of the manifested product’s collapse.

### Real Origin

In order to reify the desired product, practitioners must manifest from an origin in real space. This is due to the very definition of a manifestation technique requiring that the product must be capable of coming into existence in real space. The requirement is simultaneously a shortcut, as the innate requirement for a real origin gives practitioners an easy starting point. However, it must be noted that this requirement is yet another weakness inherent to all manifestation techniques: One cannot use a manifestation technique when there is no source from which to manifest; we can make the possible plausible, but we cannot make the impossible possible.

## Applications

Depending on the scope of the reification, manifestation techniques apply differently based on whether it is a partial or direct manifestation.

### Partial Manifestation

When manifesting concepts that are unwieldy (as is common with most usages of Green manifestation techniques), a partial manifestation is ideal. With partial manifestation, an additional sign in the technique is required as synecdoche to control the scope and level of manifestation. As such, activation of a partial manifestation technique usually uses spontaneous and percussive signs that directly limit the duration and magnitude of the partial manifestation.

  

Partial manifestation is commonly used to invoke the clause of a [[Structural Technique]] and bring the product into existence, as is in the case of summoning techniques. It is also common to see partial manifestation used in high-energy techniques, where the reification of extraordinarily energetic events are required to produce another product. In addition, practitioners may use partial manifestation techniques as a shorthand for a more costly or exhaustive manifestation technique.

### Direct Manifestation

Direct manifestation requires little in terms of the number of communicable signs to activate, but also brushes dangerously with Color saturation and technique exhaustion, being the second most exhaustive technique class of all cataphatic techniques beside creation techniques. Practitioners often enclose a directly manifested product within a [[Barrier Technique |barrier]] due to the implausible nature of the product, either to reduce collateral damage or to prevent further exhaustion.

  

Direct manifestations are seldom used in typical summoning rites, and are usually reserved for an emergency clause. Instead, direct manifestation most often appears in research settings, where many experiments require extremely rare or novel conditions to even execute. Outside of scholarly settings, practitioners also use direct manifestations as a driver for other techniques, using the manifested product as a basis from which other techniques can stem.

## Mechanical Considerations

Manifestation techniques are a powerful class of cataphatic techniques that find heavy use with more abstract concepts like infinity, potential, etc. As such, they are great to deploy as drivers and buffers of any other technique, or simply as a powerful attack or defense in and of itself.
