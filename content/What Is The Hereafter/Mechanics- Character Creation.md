# The Basics

## Create a character!

No, literally. Create a character. No need to read the lore if you don’t want to. Your character doesn’t even need to come from the vertex world at all. Be you from magical world of might and magic or a concrete jungle of godless heathens, this world has a place for you!

  

Have an OC you want to transfer over? That’s cool too! Let me know and we can transfer your character.

## Irregularity

Player characters are unique to the world of The Hereafter in more ways than just their character. Each character also has access to a unique ability known as their Core Skill. This ability has the unique distinction of being a universal power that can interact with every aspect of the mechanics, so make it powerful and fun!

  

## Stats, Attributes, and Traits

Your characters start with a set amount of the stats Physique (PHY), Intelligence (INT), Instinct (INS), Precision (PRC), and Agility (AGI), along with certain traits that affect the gameplay of the character (if they are required to complete the character). As you play through The Hereafter, each of these stats will passively react and comment on your current situation, sometimes offering helpful (or not) advice or providing much needed characterization and detail. The higher a stat becomes, the more it likes to chime in, and the more often it tries to get you to try something. For example, having a high INT stat will grant you frequent clues and hints to solve current issues, while having a high PRC stat might make you try your best and acting and performing great feats of self-control. Of the five core stats, only PHY is not typically involved in stat checks.

The core five stats do not increase through a direct progression system, instead being rewarded for in-character development and the acquisition of new traits. As the stat gets higher and higher, the more difficult it will be to attain more point in that stat, usually topping out around 20-25 before natural acquisition is no longer feasible.

  

It is highly recommended to give a starting stat of at least 10 to your new characters if you wish to lean into that aspect of the related stat. Beyond the four major checked stats, there are three further stats that are closer in progression to other RPGs.

## In Rainbows

Beside the seven overall stats, there are also the seven Color Affinities. Color is the categorization of unique powers in The Hereafter, with each of the seven Colors centered around an operating theme that further subdivides as your understanding of Color increases. Like the four often-checked stats, Color Affinity increases primarily from exploration and innovation, and provide valuable insight into the world, especially after the stat goes above 15.
