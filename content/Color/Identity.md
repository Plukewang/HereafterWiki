#color #red

The Color of self-sameness, origins, and death. The domains of Red are the domains of all things singular. 

---
## Domain

The domain of Red is **Null Space**, comprising a spectrum of ontologies equipped with an identity—that is, *a communicable sign within the ontology that maps onto itself.* This takes the forms of abstract concepts like nihility, zero, and death, but also maps onto any singular definitions. In this sense, it is the most reductive of all Colors, as all things within Null Space is derived from the reduction in complexity from other domains of the Upper Cosm.

Subdomains of Null Space include, but are not limited to:

- The Vermillion (Scalar Space): Signs represented by comparable singular properties. These include simple observables like velocity, distance, and even time, so long as its specification has no external and composite parts.
  
- The Carmine (Alias Space): Compartmentalized signs whose complexities are encapsulated together in a unit. Names, especially categorical ones, fall into this subdomain, alongside objects with well-defined substructures that nonetheless appear whole like atoms.
  
- The Scarlet (Delirium Space): Signs that cover and reduce other signs to itself. "Grey goo" concepts that both self-propagate and convert other things to an instance of itself are common in the subdomain. Examples include most self-replicating disease vectors from viruses and prions to social memes and thought-terminating clichés.
  
- The Rouge (Origin Space): Signs that act as terminal points in processes. Death and all deathlike phenomena fall under this domain. Homes, sinks, centers of whirlpools, and black holes are common examples.
  
- The Maroon (Prototype Space): Definitional signs. All signs within this subdomain serve as fundamental points of comparison to more defined and concrete signs. All examples are idealized forms, as this subdomain is particularly decoupled from concrete space.
  
- The Crimson (Precursor Space): The unspeakable signs, the oldest and most inscrutable whispers of the Upper Cosm.
  
- The Roseate (Forbidden Space) Inaccessible signs within the Upper Cosm. 

Of these, the Vermillion, the Carmine, and the Scarlet are used cataphatically, while the Rouge and the Maroon are typically involved in apophatic techniques. The Crimson and the Roseate, while under research, are poorly understood.
