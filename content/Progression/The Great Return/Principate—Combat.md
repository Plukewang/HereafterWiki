#progression

Practitioners with at least [[Phantasm Proficiency#**Phantasm Proficiency III**|Phantasm Proficiency III]] can learn the following techniques.

## Living Force

#### **Configuration: Melancholy**

> Exort toward a central point of impact around which one generates an outwardly-expanding rotating [[Instance|instance]] with a maximum range multiplied by 1x highest affinity tier. The expanding rotational [[Gradient|gradient]] reduces effectiveness within the very center to 1 but increases effectiveness of *Amplify* by 1x highest affinity tier at the expanding edge. 
> 
> Techniques utilizing *Pierce* have 1x reduced effectiveness toward this instance.

The melancholy configuration appears like an expanding, rotating sphere of distorted light that obscures almost everything within, even to an experienced practitioner. The massive amplification of effectiveness often manifests as immense and total damage as the distorted wall of light expands outward and dissipates. At higher levels of PHY, nothing but dust will remain in the effective range of the instance. As the instance does not possess a barrier, anti-barrier techniques are ineffective.

#### **Configuration: Languor**

>Targeting an existing barrier within which one utilizes living force, imbue the barrier with rotation while shrinking the size of the barrier as the action occurs, causing the action and the barrier to simultaneously hit. Effectiveness reduction effects must first overcome the barrier's defense. 
>
>If the barrier is destroyed, the technique becomes standard living force without penalty save those associated with the barrier's destruction.

Actions performed with the languor configuration become enshrouded by the rapidly-shrinking barrier, momentarily turning entirely opaque on the moment before impact. While the technique does not necessarily hit harder, it still serves as a layer of defense against disruption from other practitioners, providing another point of failure that must go before the technique can be fully disrupted. It is possible to bypass the barrier to disrupt the internalized technique, but this does not prevent the barrier from activating the imbued effect itself. 

Should the barrier and living force hits not land simultaneously, the time lag can allow a practitioner to react and dispel the living force.
