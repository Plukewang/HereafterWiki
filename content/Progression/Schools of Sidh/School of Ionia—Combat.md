#progression 

As long as the practitioner may partition and control instances, they may learn and acquire the following techniques:
## Living Force

#### **Cavitating Force**

> Create a partitioned instance from the trajectory of one’s motion or weapon. Unlike motile force, cavitating force instances can take any shape that the practitioner may create. The practitioner can control the initial shape and velocities. 

 Cavitating force creates instances as if they were bubbles boiling off from the central fluid of the phantasm, and generally have the same visibility as all living force.

#### **Redoubled Force**

>Create a partitioned instance that duplicates the original attack’s properties and repeats its effects upon activation. The partition is created at the same time as the original like cavitating force, but does not linger.>

Redoubled force produces a secondary impression of the original action, but can be projected in any direction around the practitioner. While it follows typical living force visibility rules, it is almost always visible when the instance is overlaid over the original action directly. 

#### **Perturbing Force**

> Create intentional disturbances within a smooth motion to launch a nebula of minor partitioned instances. Instances created by this technique cannot be directly controlled within the scope of this technique and cannot re-apply effects. In addition, effectiveness of any instances that apply to the same target is reduced to 1 after the first.

Unlike other forms of living force, perturbing force always produces visible distortions in the form of swirling vortices that appear like whorls of smoke and fog. As the smaller cloud of instances are formed, the vortices bud off and produce smaller spherical instances  that scatter along the trajectory of the action and fan outward, dissipating rapidly.
