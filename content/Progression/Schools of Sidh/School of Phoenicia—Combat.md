#progression 

As long as the practitioner may partition and control instances, they may learn and acquire the following techniques:
## Living Force

#### **Dispersive Force**

> Exort with a sharp-edge phantasm with a barrier, striking like the tip of a spear and dispersing any penetrated Color and Living Force away from the user of this skill. 
> 
> This technique gains 1x current affinity effectiveness against [[Barrier Technique#Boundary Continuity|simple barrier and transient barriers]].

Attacks made with dispersive force appear at minimum as a sharp lens distortion emanating from the line or point of the actual edge. At its densest, as in the form of the jet, the distortion becomes nearly if not fully opaque and becomes entirely black. When dispersive force strikes a barrier, shattering the barrier will cause the barrier to pop like a soap balloon. When it strikes living force, the dispersive force becomes a wedge that literally disperses the target like it were split by the edge.

#### **Plosive Force**

> Exort into a expanding phantasm, driving the gradient caught in the cone away and outward. 
> 
> This technique gains 1x current affinity effectiveness against [[Domain Technique|domain techniques]]. Those whose phantasms are scattered by this technique find it difficult to recover and require an action to retract their phantasm.

Plosive force is generally less visible than other living force techniques, being distinguishable primarily by its effects against valid targets. Its action parts the target in an manner like unseaming a thread, and the force pushing against the sides of the expansion zone produces a darkening effect while the center momentarily experiences a vacuum if there is an atmosphere.

#### **Collapsing Force**

> Exort into a contracting form,  pressing the gradient within the soft-edge inward in a directed attack. 
> 
> This technique gains 1x current affinity effectiveness against domain techniques and *Guard Crush.*

Collapsing Force can be visualized as a particularly opaque technique with a soft edge that appears to absorb light and Color when it demonstrates its effectiveness against domain techniques. 

#### **Imploding Force**

> With a flowing, rebounding technique, alter an attack to redouble onto the opponent with Living Force by expelling gradient outward, which leads to a rapid imploding secondary hit that  gains 1x current affinity effectiveness but does not reapply effects. The momentarily reduced gradient reduces effectiveness to 1.

The momentary weakness produced by the weak gradient necessary to produce the implosion that follows causes a zone of silence and stillness that only lasts a fraction of a second, followed by an outwardly-expanding sphere of reduced visibility that can be seen by almost anyone. Even with available reactions, tier 5 PRC is required to attempt to act within the window of the vacuum.

#### **Rupturing Force**

> Direct or produce an instance at the boundary of a barrier that allows for passage of gradients and enclose the instance in the barrier. Enclose Living Force within this instance, disrupting the local function of a barrier and allowing certain effects to penetrate barriers that otherwise would prevent them from occurring. At the start of each action, check current or highest affinity against the effectiveness of the barrier to continue disrupting the barrier.

Rupturing force, when used directly on a barrier, produces a rapidly-opening hole equal to the shape of the instance disrupting the barrier. If the instance accompanies a weapon, the hole's size will constrict around the dimensions of the weapon. If barrier conditions change at any point, surjective force will fail momentarily, and a reactive reactivation will be needed to continue disrupting the barrier.
