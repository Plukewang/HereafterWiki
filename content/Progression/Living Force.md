#apophatic #progression

Living force is a primarily [[Domain Technique |domain-based technique]] used to amplify actions' effectiveness.

> *With one’s phantasm, imbue one’s weapon with one’s will while striking, resulting in attacks with greatly multiplied force.

Physical actions performed with the kinetic chain of the body gain vastly increased effectiveness while a practitioner engages this technique. As such, it is a foundational technique within all traditions and the root from which all offensive physical techniques spring.

## Description

**Living Force**

> Grants the *Amplify* type, increasing physical actions' effectiveness. If phase distortion is active, gain 1x current affinity as effectiveness, otherwise, gain 1x highest affinity instead. All evolutions of living force automatically apply *Amplify.*

Living force manifests in inexperienced practitioners as a visible distortion akin to heat waves around the body and attached objects while active and do not manifest observably at all in experienced practitioners. While improved control can direct the additional energy provided by this technique, its raw form simply adds to the existing amount purely as an addition. As such, at the basic level, the effect of amplification via living force should be depicted simply as a stronger version of the original action.

## Evolution

From the beginning, practitioners gain access to a basic set of additional variations on Living Force.

#### **Projected Force**

> Focus and extend the line of offense from one’s weapon past its physical dimensions, increasing the reach of the strike with the power of living force. The action's range is multiplied by 1x highest affinity.

 Projected force appears similarly to the base form of living force, as a visible distortion but extended along the full trajectory of the extended action. As with the basic technique, projected force becomes less perceptible as the practitioner becomes more experienced. It should be noted that the extension of range obeys physical laws, and so act as if the dimension of the action being performed had been instantly extended in the line of action. This implies that the extended action will behave the same and have similar trajectories at its edge and at its start.

#### **Motile Force**

> Focus and send forth the impression of a strike in a phantasm instance, preserving the direct impact of the attack over longer ranges and durations. The instance moves 1x highest affinity times the movement speed of the practitioner.

 Motile force appears as a projectile taking the general impression of the line of action's trajectory. Attacks swung in arcs will appear as arcs, and straight lines will appear as straight lines. As expected, their visibility reduces with practitioner experience. As with all instances, their range is limited, and the motile force instance will dissipate the same way. A corollary to this comparison is that the instance may be manipulated like any other instance as well.

At [[Phantasm Proficiency|Phantasm Proficiency II]], practitioners gain the ability to learn (if not previously learned) additional variations based on the three schools of Sidh.

[[School of Ionia—Combat|The School of Ionia]] provides techniques focused primarily on the creation of instances that either linger, provide support, or generally add extra effects to living force.

[[School of Phoenicia—Combat|The School of Phoenicia]] introduces concepts of simple domain and barrier techniques to living force, allowing for increased effectiveness in specific scenarios.

[[School of Sidh—Combat|The School of Sidh]] supplies barrier techniques and domain techniques that apply to instances as well as the phantasm, advancing the theories of technique imbuement.

---
At Phantasm Proficiency III, practitioners further gain access to variations based on the societies of the Great Return.

[[Principate—Combat|The Principate Sepulcher]] further refines instance manipulation, applying barrier techniques and controlling multiple instances in parallel as well as producing bizarre geometries with the domain.

The Holdfasts develops living force techniques that further enhance the effectiveness of certain actions through gestures and incantations while also advancing the subfield of gradient techniques.

The Magellanic Exile Fleet establishes new techniques that advance the School of Sidh's developments by accounting for more complex barrier-barrier and domain-barrier interactions that occur during living force application.

The Starborne conceptualize specialized forms of domain techniques that focus on manipulation of spatial geometry and extreme variations in range and strength.

At this stage, personal development of individualized living force techniques begin.
